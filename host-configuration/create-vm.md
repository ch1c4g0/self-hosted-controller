

<p><h1>Preperation</h1></p>

<p>We need to upload our image into our server, in the case that you have more than one, make sure you upload your image into the correct server.</p>

1. Click on your servers name under datacenter
2. click local(server name here)
3. Click on ISO Images
4. Upload your downloaded image or use the Upload for URL feature.
5. Wait for "TASK OK:"


<p><h1>Creating the VM</h1></p>

1. CLick on your database
2. Click Create VM in the top right
3. Choose your VM-ID (110 for me)
4. Name your VM (SEC01-US01)
5. Skip the "SYSTEM" step, defaults are fine,
6. Add your Disk Size, the rest can be default.
7. Set your cores (I used 3)
8. Set your Memory (I used 4.0GB)
9. Leave network tab default.
10. Confirm
11. Click on your new VM in the left-hand side.
12. Power it on in the top-right
